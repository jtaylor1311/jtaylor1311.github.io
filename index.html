My Services

Business Review, Analysis & Enablement

Many business processes and day-to-day workings are shrouded in mystery. I take the initiative to understand, document and visualise all aspects of your organisation's operations and provide suggestions for improvements. I also provide employees with the tools to manage documentation themselves to track changes and reduce training time.

Process Analysis, Improvement & Automation

In the digital age, many organisations are not optimised and perform processes in very unstructured and manual ways. Taking the time to review key processes, I provide documentation of the end-to-end processes and provide both process and automation suggestions to suit your priorities.

Stakeholder Engagement & Project Management

I've been involved in several high-value projects, providing updates and support to both business and technology-focused teams. This has taught me the value in aligning expectations, motivating through shared common value and celebrating everyone on the team. I am also educated in Agile, Waterfall and Prince2 project management methodologies and have had to manage various PM responsibilities in my roles.
